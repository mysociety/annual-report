Yes, it’s that time of year again.

Time to throw another log on the fire, cosy up to someone you love, and take a minute or two to see what mySociety have been up to since our last annual report.

For starters:

Have you seen our new mission statement? It’s only six words long, but we think it says it all:

We help people be active citizens

In the UK, and internationally; in people’s dealings with public authorities, politicians, and those who wield power; and across the three areas of Transparency, Community and Democracy; through the digital tools, research, datasets and events that we create and share, that’s what we do.
