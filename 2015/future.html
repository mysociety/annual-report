That was mySociety in 2015.

We did plenty. But our ambitions are even bigger.

We want to establish mySociety as a leader in international civic tech, providing global direction and inspiration.

In 2016, we’ll be improving and extending our work in our key areas—freedom of information, parliaments & elections, and local issue reporting—with new tools, new services and new collaborations.

Right now, we have partners in 35 countries around the world. Watch this space as we work to increase that figure – and extend our reach in the UK, too.

This report is dedicated to our supporters and funders. We couldn’t do any of this work without the support they provide.
