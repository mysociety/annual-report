How you help

Whether you’re a funder, a donor, a volunteer or someone who visits our sites, we couldn’t do it without you.

We want to give you a great big, heartfelt ‘thank you.’

  • The William and Flora Hewlett Foundation, University College London and Microsoft — for funding our research.
